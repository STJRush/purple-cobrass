Telsa Model 3 Start off at €33500 2017

The model 3 is designed to attain the highest safety ratings in every category, Model 3 achieves 220 miles of range while starting at only 35,000 USD before incentives. Model 3 is a smaller, simpler, more affordable electric car. Although it is Tesla's newest vehicle, Model 3 is not “Version 3” or the most advanced Tesla. Like Model S, it is designed to be the safest car in its class.

Model 3 has seating for 5 adults. The cargo for the model 3 is 15 cu ft with Front & Rear Trunk Cargo Volume with Manual Trunk. Model 3 Specs for safety are automatic emergency braking and collision avoidance,Eight airbags and Electronic stability and traction control. Specs for convenience are 15″ touchscreen with onboard maps & navigation,Wi-Fi and LTE internet connectivity, Keyless entry and remote climate control with app,Voice activated controls and 60/40 split folding rear seats. The Standard Interior is 15” touchscreen display,Dual zone climate control system,FM/Internet streaming radio, Textile seating and Center console with open storage and two USB ports.
